  • Overview

    Datt Velpore is a surgical paper tape. It is hypoallergenic, 100% latex free, non woven, lightweight, conformable, water repellent and tearable by hand. Velpore is porous and allows the skin to breathe. Velpore is white in colour

  • Ussage

    Velpore can be used for securing dressings, tubes and catheters to the body

  • Specifications

    Velpore dispenser packs are available in the following configurations: - 1 Inch X 10 Yards (9.14 m) in a box of 12 rolls with dispenser - 2 Inch X 10 Yards (9.14 m) in a box of 6 rolls with dispenser - 3 Inch X 10 Yards (9.14 m) in a box of 4 rolls with dispenser

  • Precautions

    Avoid Sunlight, store at room temperature
